Bronco Hack 2016: ESEMES

Our team of 5 programmers and 1 business student took first place for ESEMES, a web application written in Python which enables people to receive web information such as directions, Yelp suggestions, and wikipedia searches through text message. Our application was aimed to service communities with restricted access to cellular data or people who could not afford to buy data plans.
